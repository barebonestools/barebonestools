About/FAQ

Why does it say "Invalid inputs"?

  1. The lower limit is greater than the upper limit.
  2. The number of random numbers to generate is less than zero.
  3. There are decimals, which are not currently supported (shoot me an email and I'll add it!)

What does inclusive mean?

Inclusive means that the lower and upper limits are included in the range of numbers that can be generated. For example, if you set the lower limit to 1 and the upper limit to 10, the random number generator can generate any number from 1 to 10, including both 1 and 10.

Are there any keyboard shortcuts?

Yes, you can use [Tab] and [Shift]+[Tab] to navigate between the input fields, the links, and the buttons and [Enter] to either click the current (focused) link/button or click the go button, if the focus is not on a button/link.
